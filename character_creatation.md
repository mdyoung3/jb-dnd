## **Card 1 — Choose Class**
- Pick class first (it guides all other choices)
- Determines:
  - Main ability score(s)
  - Hit Die (for HP)
  - Saving throw proficiencies
  - Skill proficiencies (choose from list)
  - Weapon, armor, and tool proficiencies
  - Level 1 class features (e.g., Rage, Spellcasting)
  - Starting equipment options
- Start here so you know what stats matter

---

## **Card 2 — Choose Race / Lineage**
- Provides:
  - Ability score bonuses (unless using custom origins)
  - Walking speed
  - Racial features:
    - Darkvision
    - Damage resistances
    - Skill proficiencies
    - Extra spells or abilities
  - Languages
- Some races sys use:
  - Ability mod + proficiency bonus
  - Proficiency bonus at level 1 = **+2**

---

## **Card 6 — Hit Points**
- Level 1 HP = Hit Die maximum + CON modifier
  - Fighter = 10 + CON
  - Cleric/Rogue = 8 + CON
  - Wizard = 6 + CON
- Write max HP clearly

---

## **Card 7 — Armor Class & Initiative**
**Armor Class:**
- Wearing armor → armor AC rules + DEX (light/medium)
- No armor → 10 + DEX
- Shields = +2 AC

**Initiative:**
- Initiative = DEX modifier

---

## **Card 8 — Attacks**
For each weapon:
- Attack Bonus = ability mod (STR/DEX) + proficiency bonus
- Damage = weapon die + ability modifier
- Note:
  - Finesse weapons can use STR or DEX
  - Ranged weapons use DEX
